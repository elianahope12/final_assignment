Figure Skating is a very popular winter olympic sport. Countries take their team qualifiers very seriously to guarantee that the best athletes arrive on the world stage in February. The rankings are decided by ISU using the total points of skaters from their past 3 seasons. Not all competitions are given the same amount of weight and the value of the points won goes down year to year. This scoring system is difficult to understand at a first glance, but a more in depth overview can be found here.
